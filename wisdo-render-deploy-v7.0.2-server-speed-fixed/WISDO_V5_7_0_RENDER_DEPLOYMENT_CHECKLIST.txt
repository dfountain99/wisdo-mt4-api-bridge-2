WISDO v5.7.0 Render Deployment Checklist

1. Deploy the latest GitHub main commit containing package version 5.7.0.
2. Keep existing Resend and Twilio environment variables.
3. Add a long random WISDO_LEAD_PORTAL_SECRET.
4. Keep WISDO_LEAD_PORTAL_TTL_DAYS=30 unless another expiry is required.
5. Keep WISDO_FUNNEL_SEQUENCE_STEP_MINUTES=0 in production.
6. Confirm PUBLIC_BASE_URL is the live HTTPS domain.
7. Confirm persistent storage remains mounted and WISDO_STORAGE_PATH points to it.
8. Open /api/public/health and confirm version 5.7.0 plus:
   personalLearningRoom=true
   educationDripSequence=true
   portableLeadAi=true
   videoEngagementTracking=true
9. Submit a test lead with marketing consent.
10. Confirm the response redirects to /learn/<signed-token>.
11. Confirm the immediate email includes the personal learning-room link.
12. Confirm four future marketing_education messages appear in the notification outbox.
13. Open the learning room and verify webinar, video, and WISDO AI sections.
14. Ask WISDO AI a question, change pages, and confirm history persists.
15. Use the unsubscribe link and confirm scheduled education messages become cancelled.
16. Confirm /admin/growth-funnel shows engagement and sequence metrics.
